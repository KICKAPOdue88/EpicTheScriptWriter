BIO



Epic aka The ScriptWriter is an Eastern Cape(South Africa) mc, currently based in Port Elizabeth. Epic has one 4/5 star hype magazine rated EP(extended play) tilted 'Epic's Cinematography' and has recently released his much anticipated debut album titled 'Green' and collaborate project with Chap Rowland titled “One Day”.Epic Has worked with Ian Kamau, Gobi Beast, Nyambz, Big Zulu, K2 to mention a few and shared stages with Mobb Deep, Casper Nyovest, Knaan, AKA, Black Rock, Driemanskap, Ill Skillz, Reason, Max normal(now known as DieAntwoord),MXO,The Muffinz and many more. Performed in major events such as the South African Hip Hop Awards, Sprite uncontainable shows, FIFA world cup,Splash Festival in PE, Grahamstown Arts festival. The Scriptwriter has had music played on major platforms in and outside south africa and was the most consistent winner on Y Fm stand your Ground show with Dj Mulo.
